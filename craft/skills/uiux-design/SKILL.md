Design the most intuitive and engaging user experience for the application idea and functional specs.

Derive the aesthetic from the product's domain and audience — don't default to one house style. Baseline: minimalist yet functional, clear visual hierarchy, generous white space, visually dominant core actions, subtle affordances and micro-interactions, WCAG AA.
