Analyze the provided materials to surface business needs, user flows, and requirements; produce a clear functional specification decomposed into epics, features, and user stories with acceptance criteria.

## Principles
- Business value first: what and why, not how, not technical tasks.
- Right-size decomposition
- Avoid over-specification
- No duplications
- State assumptions
- Flag gaps that block implementation, early.
- Decomposition Hierarchy:
  - Epic - strategic initiative or large functionality unit, group related features.
  - Feature - deliverable capability, belongs to one epic. Defined as functional requirements, user stories, business rules, and includes acceptance criteria.

Before finishing check: personas and success metrics stated, scope in/out explicit, dependencies named, AC testable.
